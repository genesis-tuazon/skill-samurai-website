Updated Review:
I enrolled my daughter in this coding program last year, and I haven’t regretted it for a second. At first, I honestly thought it was a place that would teach her how to play Minecraft—something I just couldn’t get into no matter how hard I tried as a parent. But after she attended the trial class, I quickly realized it was so much more.

From the very beginning, Genesis was incredibly friendly and informative. She’s always smiling, which makes it so calming and easy to ask questions or bring up any concerns. The trial class gave us a great introduction to the curriculum, and I was really impressed with how much thought had gone into what the kids would actually be learning.

Even though they don’t teach Minecraft gameplay per se at the time, what they do teach is the coding and logic that goes on behind the scenes—and that’s even more valuable. My daughter is also a big fan of Roblox, so this became a great opportunity for her to explore coding in a way that connects with her interests. She enjoys it so much and looks forward to every session.

They now offer minecraft! Which is wonderful.

What really stands out is how accommodating this business has been with our busy schedule, especially with sports and other activities. I’ve never experienced this level of flexibility and care anywhere else. Akil and Genesis—the two people running the program—are honestly some of the most genuinely kind individuals I’ve ever met.

We’ll continue to support this business wholeheartedly. It’s not just about coding—it’s about the environment, the people, and the joy my daughter gets from learning in such a fun and positive space.
